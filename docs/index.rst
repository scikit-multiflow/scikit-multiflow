Welcome to scikit-multiflow's documentation!
============================================

.. image:: _static/images/header.jpg
   :scale: 100 %
   :alt: flow
   :align: center

``scikit-multiflow`` is a multi-label and stream data framework, inspired by the
`MOA <https://moa.cms.waikato.ac.nz/>`_ and `MEKA <http://meka.sourceforge.net/>`_ projects, and following the pythonic
philosophy of `scikit-learn <http://scikit-learn.org/stable/>`_.
